Delete Your Account

You can delete your FuelWatch JA account and all associated personal data at any time. We offer two ways to do this:

Option 1 — Delete In-App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the FuelWatch app:

1
Open the FuelWatch JA app and log in
2
Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen
3
Scroll down and tap Delete Account
4
Confirm the deletion — your account and personal data will be permanently removed. Your price reports will remain to keep station history accurate for the community.

Option 2 — Request via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

hello@fuelwatchja.com

Please include the email address associated with your FuelWatch JA account. We will process your request within 7 days.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

✓  Your profile (username, email, parish, avatar)
✓  Your favourite stations and notification preferences
✓  Your rank points and leaderboard entry
✓  Any photos you submitted as proof

⚠️  Your price reports are not deleted — they remain anonymised under a [deleted] account to preserve station price history for the community.

Retention Period

All personal data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. Aggregated and anonymised pricing data (with no link to your account) may be retained to maintain historical price accuracy across the platform.
