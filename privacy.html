FuelWatch JA

Jamaica's Crowdsourced Fuel Price Tracker

Last updated: June 16, 2026

FuelWatch JA ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FuelWatch JA mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your email address and username.
  • Location data: We request your location to show nearby fuel stations and to verify that price reports are submitted from the correct location. Location is only accessed while the app is in use.
  • Price reports: When you submit a fuel price report, we store the price data, station details, timestamp, and optionally a photo of the price board.
  • Profile information: You may optionally upload a profile photo.
  • Device information: We collect push notification tokens to send you price change alerts for your saved stations.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display crowdsourced fuel prices across Jamaica
  • To verify the accuracy of price reports
  • To send push notifications about price changes at your favourite stations
  • To calculate leaderboard rankings based on your contributions
  • To improve the accuracy and reliability of the app

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your username and price contributions are visible to other users of the app as part of the crowdsourced reporting feature. Your email address is never shared publicly.

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Google Maps — map display and location services
  • Expo / EAS — app delivery and push notifications
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

Location Data

FuelWatch JA requests access to your device location while the app is in use. This is used to show nearby stations, filter results by distance, and verify that price reports are submitted from within 75 metres of the reported station. We do not track your location in the background.

Photos

Photos submitted as proof of price reports are stored securely and used solely for moderation purposes. Profile photos are stored and displayed within the app.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Price reports and history are retained indefinitely to power historical charts. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

FuelWatch JA is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections and secure cloud storage to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
