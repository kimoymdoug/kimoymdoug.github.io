❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a fuel price?

Tap the Report tab at the bottom of the app. You must be within 75 metres of the station to submit a price report.

Why can't I see any stations near me?

Make sure location permissions are enabled for FuelWatch JA in your device settings. You can also tap the 🗺️ Island button to see all stations across Jamaica.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm with a code. Your price reports will remain to keep station history accurate, but your personal data will be removed.

Why is my price report still pending?

Reports are confirmed once verified by other nearby users. Pending reports are still visible on the app — they just haven't been confirmed by the community yet.

How do I earn points and rank up?

You earn 10 points for each price report submitted and 5 points for each verification. The more accurate reports you submit, the higher your rank — from Newcomer all the way to Super Fueler.

How do I report a wrong station or incorrect information?

Open the station's detail page and tap the 🚩 Flag button. Select the issue type and submit — our team reviews all flags.

The app says I'm offline but I have internet.

This can happen on first launch. Close and reopen the app. If the issue persists, check your connection and try again.
